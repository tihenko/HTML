Sushi

  1. Rice
  2. Fish
  3. Soy sauce
  4. Cheese
  5. Sesame
  6. Cucumber
  7. Wasabi
  8. Nori

You need to take 300 g of rice, boil it for 20 minutes.
Chop the fish into small pieces.
Add cucumber, sesame seeds, cheese.
Wrap everything in Nori.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Serve with wasabi and soy sauce.
